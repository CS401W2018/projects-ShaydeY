Stitch (AKA X626)

Stitch, otherwise known as experiment 626, is an alien genetic experiment created by Joomba Jookiba. His primary function is to destroy basically everything he can. Super strong, inteligent and almost unkillable.

Lilo Pelakai

A young orphaned hawaiian girl. Lives with her older sister Nani, who does her best to take care of the both of them. Alienated by her classmates, she found family in the aliens.

Cobra Bubbles

Ex CIA agent turned social worker. Initially did not believe Nina could take care of Lilo but at the end of the movie, supports them after everything thats happened. While he was a CIA agent he convinced the aliens that mosquitoes were an endangered species to save the planet.
