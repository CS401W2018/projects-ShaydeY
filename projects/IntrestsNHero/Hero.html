Supergiant Games:

Supergiant Games is an indie game developer and publisher made up of a small team. They are most well known for their game Hades, which won many awards and is highly rated. They are also known for thier amazing art visuals, unique gameplay, and soundtrack.

Notable Citations:

Bastion
Supergiant Game's first game made. Its "A action RPG (Role Playing Game) in which you must create and fight for civilization's last refuge." Really high quality for first game published.
Hades
A rouge-like dungeon crawler. You play as Hades's rebelious son and are dropped into the middle of Greek Mythology as you try to esacape your Father's domain. Critically acclaimed. Won multiple gaming awards in 2021. Catapulted Supergiant Game's popularity.
Hades 2
Currently in early access and is not yet rated or technically complete. A sequal to Hades in which you fight the Titan of Time.
