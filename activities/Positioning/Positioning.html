Questions and Answers

What is normal flow in HTML?

HTML works and prioritizes from top bottom.

What does float do? Why do you need to clear the float?

Float allows sibling tags to share space. You need to clear float so that everything underneath the selected tag isn't automatically pulled up.

What do absolute, fixed, and relative do?

Use Relative for small minor adjustments Relative to something else; hardly ever use because of formating issue when dealing with size. Absolute will rip it out and move it exactly where you designate it; will scroll off. Fixed will place it and keep it there on your screen according to browsing window. Fixed and Absolute is commonly use in the header and footer.
