Questions and Answers

What is the difference between block tags and inline tags?

Block tags have width, height and make a box; spans the entire width but only as tall as the text put inside unless declared otherwise (Examples: div, p, header, etc); inline tags surround text in a box (Examples: span, a, img)

What is the margin, border, and padding?

Margin is space put on the outside of the box. Border is literally the border of the box itself. Padding is space put on the inside of the box

What are units of measurement you can use in CSS?

Pixels, Descriptive text (thick, thin, etc), in, cm, percentage, em

When would you use a figure tag?

When you want to put a caption on something or to make inline boxes into block boxes.
