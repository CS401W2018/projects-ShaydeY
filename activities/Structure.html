Q1: What goes in the header?

In the header, is usually the domain's logo or symbol. Also has a search bar, and sometimes navigational tabs.

Q2: What goes in the Nav? Can the Nav go in the header? Does it have to go in the header? Does the Nav contain links to another website?

In the nav, is usually your navigational tabs. This includes links to other sites in your domain or part of your company. The Nav can go in your header, but does not have to, and will not link to outside websites.

Q3: What goes in Main?

Main is the body of your webpage. Almost all the info on a page will be under the main tag.

Q4: What goes in the Footer?

In the footer are your copyright, and additional links that one might find helpful.
