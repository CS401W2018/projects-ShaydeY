Now how dangerous are explosives? Well this data comes straight from the ATF. If you don't know the ATF is the bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms, AND Explosives. It is a law enforcement agency that investigates and handles offences related to firearms, explosives, arson. They also regulate the sale (legal and illegal) of alcohol, tobacco, as well as firearms, and explosives. The data you see below you in tables have been collected from the 2023 USBDC EIR. Or in other words the 2023 United States Bomb Center Explosive Incident Report. The tables that you see here are just portions taken from their offical unclassified report. For a more comprehensive look into all the data please look at their report, which PDF I have linked at the bottom.
