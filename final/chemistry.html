This video showcases something called Nitrogen Triiodide, otherwise known as Touch Powder. One of the coolest (and relatively safe) explosives out there. Why? well watch the video, then the name and reason will make sense. Nitrogen Triiodide, which I'll just refer to as Touch Powder, is well known in the explosives world becuase of how unstable it is. As seen from the video it is an extremely sensitive contact explosive. Although it is an explosive its synthesis is actually quite simple in a classroom setting, or for anyone with the right equipment. This is a link to one such procedure walking you through it. I will second it's warning though, "Quantities must be kept small so that the demonstration is safe. Do not be tempted to deviate from the method."
